About DFC Hangul

We are a club on campus that promotes learning, understanding, and respecting Korean language and culture. Our weekly lessons are led by native Korean speakers who have lived in Korea and can not only teach you a beautiful language complete with the nuances and quirks of native speaking, but also share the richness of Korean culture.

With a wide range of levels and dialects represented, our teachers bring their joy and love for their country into each lesson. Whether you’re curious about K-Dramas, K-Pop, or simply want to broaden your global perspective, DFC Hangul is a welcoming place to learn, connect, and have fun.

Lessons & Meetings

Lessons: Every Friday in FN 2.210
Meetings: Biweekly in the same room (FN 2.210)
